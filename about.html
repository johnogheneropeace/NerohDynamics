About Neroh Dynamics

Technology in service of real problems

We are a digital technology company using applied AI to build products that improve lives, one sector at a time.

Our mission

Solve real problems with technology people can trust

We exist to close the gap between what technology can do and what communities actually need. We build AI-powered products that are practical, accessible and grounded in the realities of the people who use them.

Our vision

An Africa where good technology reaches everyone

We picture a future where a mother in a rural clinic, a student far from a city and a smallholder farmer all benefit from the same standard of thoughtful, intelligent software.

Our story

Founded to build what was missing

Neroh Dynamics began with a simple frustration. Too many of the tools that communities needed either did not exist or were built for someone else's context. We set out to change that by combining strong engineering with deep sector knowledge.

Our first flagship product, MyMeema, grew out of work in maternal and child health. It taught us how much careful design and trust matter when technology reaches families directly.

Today we work across health, education, agriculture, finance and more. The thread that connects everything is the same. We look for hard, worthwhile problems, then build AI-powered products that stand up in the field.

We are rooted in Abuja, Nigeria, and we build with an African context first. The lessons we learn here travel well.

What guides us

Principles we build by

Privacy first

Trust is the foundation of adoption, so we protect people's data as a matter of principle.

Human-centred

We design with the people who will use our products, so the result fits their lives.

Impact over hype

We measure success by real outcomes in the field, not by how impressive a demo looks.

Built to last

We engineer for reliability, so our products keep working long after launch day.
