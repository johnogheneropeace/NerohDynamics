About Neroh Dynamics

Intelligence built for Africa

We are an African artificial intelligence, data science and innovation company, building the systems institutions need to make better decisions and deliver better services.

Our mission

Put world-class intelligence to work for institutions

We exist to close the gap between what modern technology can do and what African institutions can access. We build practical, trustworthy systems grounded in the realities of the people and organisations that use them.

Our vision

A continent that leads with its own technology

We picture an Africa where governments, businesses and communities run on intelligent systems designed for their context, built to a global standard by people who understand the ground.

Our story

Founded to build what was missing

Neroh Dynamics began with a simple frustration. Too many of the systems institutions needed either did not exist or were built for someone else's context. We set out to change that by combining artificial intelligence, data science and strong engineering with deep sector knowledge.

Our flagship product, MyMeema, grew out of work in maternal and child health. It taught us how much careful design, evidence and trust matter when technology reaches people directly.

Today we work across government, healthcare, finance, agriculture, climate and the wider development sector. The thread is the same throughout. We look for hard, worthwhile problems, then build intelligent systems that stand up in the field.

We are rooted in Abuja, Nigeria, and we build with an African context first. The intelligence we develop here is made to travel across the continent.

What guides us

Principles we build by

Privacy first

Trust is the foundation of adoption, so we protect people's data as a matter of principle.

Human-centred

We design with the people who will use our products, so the result fits their lives.

Evidence over hype

We measure success by real outcomes for institutions and communities, rather than by how impressive a demo looks.

Built to scale

We engineer for reliability and growth, so systems keep working from pilot through to national adoption.
